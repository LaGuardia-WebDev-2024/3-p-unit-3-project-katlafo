General Info

  • time: 20 min
  • serves: 12 cookies
Ingredients Quantity
salted butter 8 tablespoons
white sugar 1/2 cup
light brown sugar 1/4 cup
vanilla extract 1 teaspoon
eggs just 1!
all purpose flour 1 1/2 cups
baking soda 1/2 teaspoon
salt 1/4 teaspoon
chocolate chips personal choice!

Step by Step Instructions

  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees. microwave butter for about 40 seconds to just barely melt it.

  2. using a mixer, beat the butter with the sugars until creamy.

  3. add the vanilla and egg to the mix and beat on low speed for about 10-15 seconds

  4. add flour, baking soda, and salt. mix until crumbles form.

  5. use hands to press crumbles together into a dough.

  6. add chocolate chips and mix in with your hands

  7. roll dough into 12 large balls and place onto a cookie sheet.

  8. bake for 9-11 minutes until cookies look puffy and just barely golden

  9. let cool... and then yummy yummy! enjoy!
